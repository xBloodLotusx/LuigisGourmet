
# Luigi's Gourmet Italian Restaurant — Landing Page

A warm, elegant single-page site celebrating Luigi's authentic Italian dining in Roanoke, VA.

## Design Direction
- **Palette**: Deep Italian red, warm cream/ivory background, olive green accents, charcoal text, gold highlights for elegance
- **Typography**: Elegant serif for headlines (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif for body (Inter)
- **Mood**: Cozy, classic, upscale-but-welcoming — soft shadows, generous spacing, hand-crafted feel
- **Imagery**: AI-generated photos of pasta dishes, warm dining interiors, wine, and bread

## Page Sections

1. **Sticky Navigation** — Logo, anchor links (Menu, About, Gallery, Reviews, Contact), "Reserve" button
2. **Hero** — Full-bleed dining room image with overlay
   - H1: "Authentic Italian Cuisine in the Heart of Roanoke"
   - Sub: "Family-crafted recipes, warm hospitality, and unforgettable flavors."
   - CTAs: View Menu · Reserve a Table · Call (540) 989-6277
3. **About** — Two-column: warm narrative + image of bread/wine; reputation, tradition, cozy rooms, full bar
4. **Featured Dishes** — 4-card grid with photo, name, description, "Learn More"
   - Chicken Marsala, Chicken Parmigiana, Chicken Genovese, Jeffrey's Special
5. **Why Choose Luigi's** — Icon + text grid (6 items): authentic cuisine, full bar, cozy dining, outdoor seating, reservations, vegetarian options
6. **Reviews** — 3 styled testimonial cards + rating badges (4.5★ MenuPix, 4.2★ Tripadvisor)
7. **Gallery** — Masonry/grid of dining rooms, bar, pasta, sides, desserts
8. **Hours & Location** — Two-column: address, hours (Daily 4–10 PM), phone, feature chips (parking, outdoor seating, full bar, reservations) + embedded map placeholder
9. **Final CTA Banner** — "Experience Authentic Italian Dining Tonight" + Reserve / Menu / Call buttons
10. **Footer** — Address, phone, hours, social, copyright

## Functionality
- Smooth-scroll anchor navigation
- "Call Now" buttons use `tel:` links
- "Reserve" buttons open a simple reservation dialog (name, party size, date, time, phone) with toast confirmation — front-end only
- Responsive across mobile, tablet, desktop
- Subtle fade-in animations on scroll

## Assets
- Generate 8–10 AI images: hero dining room, 4 dish photos, bread/wine still life, bar area, gallery shots
